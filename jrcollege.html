Junior College

THE GUIDANCE:

Students at the Inter level are in a confused state with physical, emotional and psychological changes adding to their growth. We at Success Junior and Degree College, having understood this, have a counselling guidance cell. The members here interact with students indiviually and find out their problems, if any and counsel them. Thus a pressure free environment is created, making learning a pleasure-filled exercise.

INTERACTION:

Parent, Student and Teacher can create way for a student’s success. Success Junior and Degree College believes that parent’s role is pivotal in guiding students. Hence, parent-teacher meetings are conducted after every term; and parents are encouraged to communicate to Principal/Teaching Staff at least once every month to assess their ward’s performance.

TECHNOLOGY:

Success Junior and Degree College ie., weekend exams and marks, attendance, progress reports etc via online process.Conducting weekend online exams to encourage revision and online marks portal for parents, parents can view their wards performance anywhere and at anytime by contacting to any working days

Bio-Metric Attendnace :

With this Wards Check-In and Out timings of the students details will be sent through SMS to the parents/Registered Numbers

ENCLOSURE:

At the time of Admission, Student must submit Original Transfer certificate, SSC Marks Sheet, Bonified certificate, 4 Colour Passport Size Photographs and 2 Set Xerox copies of certificates.

NOTE:

Students eligible for scholarships i.e, BC, SC, ST & Minorities have to submit Caste and Income certificate (Below 2 Lakh) with all essential (Document details shared by the collge Administration)
