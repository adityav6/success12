Discipline and Conduct :

The Principal has complete power over the maintenance of discipline in the college

The principal may impose a fine, suspend or expel a student from the college in case, of indiscipline, violent misbehavior or gross insubordination and violation of college rules and regulation.

A student who is irregular in attendance is liable to forfeit his claim towards scholarship Other concessions like Bus Passes and Railway

  • Eligibility to represent the college/ University in sports and games
  • The principal may discontinue or cancel scholarship or any Financial Aid to a student on grounds of irregularity, indiscipline or indecent behavior.
  • In case of indiscipline of the students, the Principal will contact the parents and request them to correct their erring ward.
  • Parents are required to keep in touch with the Principal regularly and keep themselves informed regarding the progress, punctuality and regularity in attendance of their wards.
  • Students are expected to keep the college campus neat and clean, writing on the walls and damaging the college property is strictly prohibited.
  • The students should be polite and courteous to the teaching and office Staff.
  • Bringing outsiders into the college premises without obtaining prior permission from the principal is deemed to be misconduct.
  • The Hall-Ticket for the University Exams will be issued to the student only after she submits a no-dues certificate, signed by the authority concerned.
  • All library books have to be returned for obtaining no dues clearance from the library, unless the principal permits retention of books on specific conditions.
