Foundation

Hyderabad has seen a host of junior colleges mushrooming in every nook and corner of the city and the state. This puts every anxious parent in a fix with regard to the choice of a good college. To fulfil the needs of parents and students a team of Senior lecturers from the reputed College have decided to establish a college under the able leadership of Mr. L.V. Ramana Reddy, Director a renowned faculty in Physics IIT-JEE, Dr.S.Shravan Kumar Director a renowedfaculty in Sanskrit and Dr. P.Vishnu Murthy Director a renowned faculty in Zoology. NEW ERA Junior College is established with a motive to provide quality education with values to the pupils to fulfill their dreams into reality.

"A building stands on a foundation. It derives its strength from its base."

NEW ERA stands on a very firm base. Its strength is derived from its faculty with more than 15 yrs of teaching experience in IIT-JEE, EAMCET, NEET, AIIMS, BITSAT, CPT, CLAT, INTER and other competitive exams.

Differential Edge:

The differential edge of NEW ERA lies in its systematic and methodical academic schedule, benefitting students in all spheres of activity. The college armed with dedicated, academically brilliant staff has proved beneficial to students. Maintaining a friendly rapport with students has reaped benefits.
