Junior Admission

Infrastructure :

Students at the Inter level are in a confused state with physical, emotional and psychological changes adding to their growth. We at SUCCESS JUNIOR COLLEGE, having understood this, have a counselling guidance cell. The members here interact with students, find out their problems, if any and counsel them. Thus a pressure free environment is created, making learning a pleasure-filled exercise.

INTERACTION:

Parent, Student and Teacher can pave way for a student’s success. SUCCESS JUNIOR COLLEGE believes that parent’s role is pivotal in guiding students. Hence, parent-teacher meetings are conducted after every term; and parents are encouraged to meet the Principal at least once every 15 days to assess their ward’s performance.

TECHNOLOGY:

SUCCESS JUNIOR COLLEGE sends the reports through online ie., weekend marks, attendance, progress reports etc., Parents can view their wards performance anywhere and at anytime in our website:www.newerajrcollege.org

Bio-Metric Attendnace :

With this IN and OUT timings of the students will be sent through SMS to the parents everday.

ENCLOSURE:

At the time of admission, student must submit original TC, Memorandum of marks, 2 black and white, 3 colour passport size photographs and 2 set Xerox copies of certificates along with the application form without fail.

NOTE:

Students who are eligible for scholarships i.e, BC, SC, ST & Minorities have to submit caste and income certificate (below 1 Lakh) whenever it is essential.
