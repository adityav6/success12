Counselling

Counselling

PARENTS MEETING:

  • Parents meeting will be conducted 3 times in a year i.e. before Quarterly before Half-yearly and before Pre-finals.
  • Parents are humbly requested to meet the college management on any working day between 4.00 PM & 6.00 PM atleast once in a month.

PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT:

  • Personality development classes will be conducted by eminent scholars like Dr.B.V.Pattabhi Ram garu Yandamoori Veerendranath garu and others for the overall development of the student.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR STUDENTS

  • Students have to carry the identity card to college everyday and must reach the college at least before 5 minutes of the scheduled college timing.
  • Student should be regular to the classes and the tests conducted by the College.
  • Student has to maintain an attendance of 90%.
  • In case of absence for one day students have to bring a leave letter duly signed by the parent, the next day.
  • In case of absence for more than two days, parent has to accompany the ward to college to inform the authorities.
  • Students are not allowed to carry cell phones/walkman etc., during college hours. Any student found violating this rule is liable to be fined or suspended.
  • Every student has to come to college decently dressed.
  • Printed, collarless T-shirts are not allowed.
  • Any sort of indiscipline will be seriously dealt with and admission is liable to be cancelled.
  • Fee once paid will not be refunded.
  • Fee concessions apply only for first year. Concession in second year depends upon first year marks.
  • If any student wants to leave before the college hours, he/she should take the permission of the Principal as well as parents.
  • In case a student is absent continuously for a week days, without intimation, college has the right to cancel his/her admission.
  • Hall tickets will be issued only after the clearance of entire college fee.
