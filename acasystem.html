Academic System

EXAMINATIONS:

Examinations will be conducted time to time to know the performance of the student


  • IPE Weekend Exam for Integrated students will be conducted on every Saturday from 8:30 AM to 11: 30AM.
  • IPE Weekend Exam for Regular, MEC&CEC students will be conduced on every Monday from 8:30 AM To 11:30 AM
  • AIEEE &EAMCET Weekend Test will be on every Monday from 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM.
  • Language Test will be on every Thursday. For Integrated from 4:00 PM to 5:10 PM. for Regular, MEC and CEC from 1:30PM TO 2:30PM
  • Quarterly examination will be in the month of September 1st week.
  • Half-Yearly examination will be in the month of December 1st week.
  • 2 Pre-Finals will be conducted in Is* week & 4* week of February.

NOTE: On Monday and Saturday for Integrated classes commence from 11:40 AM to 5:40 PM For Regular, MEC and CEC classes from 11:40 AM to 4:00 PM (8.30AM to 11.30AM Examination)

REPORT CARD:

  • Students weekend marks will be messaged every week.
  • Students report card will be sent home once in a month.
  • A copy of report will be handed over to the student.
  • Parents can view their wards report on our website "newerajrcollege.org"

REVISION: Intermediate syllabus revision for Integrated commence from 3rd week of January and for Regular, MEC and CEC from 1st week of January till 1st week of February.

COUNSELLING:

  • For Integrated 4PM to 6 PM everyday
  • For Regular 2PM to 4 PM everyday
  • For MEC and CEC 1:30 PM to 3 PM everyday Students will be counselled every week after the weekend results personally for the better performance in coming weekend exams.

    • DOUBTS CLARIFICATION:
    Students can get their doubts clarified from 4 PM to 6 PM everyday except on Monday and Saturday.
    For Regular, MEC and CEC students doubts clarification Timings are from 1:30 PM to 3:30 PM everyday except on Monday

    Note: Extra classes for slow learners between 4PM & 6 PM [Integrated] and 1.30 PM & 3.30 PM
    [Regular, MEC and CEC] everyday to improve their standards, similarly meritorious students will be encouraged to reach higher standards.

    DURATION: Each class is for 50 minutes and 10 minutes break will be given after every 2 classes.

    LUNCH HOUR: 12:50 PM to 1:20 PM

    • Students must carry their lunch boxes.
    • No student is allowed to leave the college premises during lunch hour.
    • Parents can drop their ward's lunch boxes by writing their name on the box and can hand over in the office.

    ABSENTISM:

    • In case of student's absence to college parents should inform to the college or must send a leave letter with the students on the next working day.
    • Student's absence will be informed to the parents on phone or the management may send a message to the number given for communication purpose.
    NOTE: If necessary the college timings may be changed but it will be informed to the parents time to time.
