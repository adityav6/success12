Academic System

 

Academic System

EXAMINATIONS:
Examinations will be conducted time to time to know the performance of the student

  • IPE Weekend Exam for Integrated students will be conducted on every Friday from 11:00 AM to 12: 30AM.
  • Online Weekend multiple choice questions Examination on Regular basis for MPC&BiPC students will be conduced on every Sunday which makes the students to revise the whole topic on regarlar basis
  • Quarterly Examination will be in the month of September 1st week.
  • Half-Yearly examination will be in the month of December 1st week.
  • Pre-Finals will be conducted in Is* week & 4* week of February. To make sure students to give their best at Final Examination which will be conducted by State Board Examination

NOTE: On Monday and Saturday for Integrated classes commence from 11:40 AM to 5:40 PM For Regular, MEC and CEC classes from 11:40 AM to 4:00 PM (8.30AM to 11.30AM Examination)

REPORT CARD:

  • Students weekend marks will be messaged every week via SMS or Calls
  • Students report card will be sent home once in a month
  • Students will be provided a copy of their marks
  • Parents can view their wards report on by visiting to the college. Our faculty members are always available to interact to the Parents/Gardians of the ward and get any information of his/her performance

REVISION: Intermediate syllabus revision for Integrated commence from 3rd week of January and for Regular, MPC, BiPC and CEC from January 1st week onwards till February last week conducting Surprise test on regular basis .

COUNSELLING:

  • For Integrated 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM on working days
  • For Regular 2PM to 4 PM everyday
  • For MEC and CEC 1:30 PM to 3 PM everyday Students will be counselled every week after the weekend results personally for the better performance in coming weekend exams.

    • DOUBTS CLARIFICATION:
    Students can get their doubts clarified from 4 PM to 6 PM everyday except on Monday and Saturday.
    For Regular, MPC, BiPC and CEC students doubts clarification Timings are from 1:30 PM to 3:30 PM everyday at college hours post class

    Note: Extra classes will be conducted for slow learners between 5M & 6 PM [Integrated] and 1.30 PM & 3.30 PM
    [Regular, MEC and CEC] everyday to improve their standards, similarly meritorious students will be encouraged to reach higher standards.

    DURATION: Each class is for 50 minutes and 10 minutes break will be given after every 2 classes.

    LUNCH HOUR: 12:50 PM to 1:20 PM

    • All Students must carry their lunch boxes.
    • No student is allowed to leave the college premises during lunch hour.
    • Parents can drop their ward's lunch boxes by writing their name along with Roll number on the box and hand over in the Front office/Reception

    ABSENTISM:

    • In case of student's absence to college parents should inform to the college or must send a leave letter with the students on the next following working day.
    • Student's remained absence will be informed to the parents on Phone/Send a Message to the number given for communication purpose
    NOTE: If necessary the college timings may be changed but it will be informed to the parents time to time.
