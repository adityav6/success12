Academic System

EXAMINATIONS:
Examinations will be conducted time to time to know the performance of the student

  • IPE Weekend Exam for Integrated students will be conducted on every Friday from 11:00 AM to 12: 30AM.
  • Online Weekend multiple choice questions Examination on Regular basis for MPC & BiPC students will be conduced on every Sunday which makes the students to revise the whole topic on regarlar basis.
  • Quarterly Examination will be in the month of September.
  • Half-Yearly examination will be in the month of December.
  • Pre-Finals will be conducted in 2'nd & 4'th week of February. To make sure students the give their best at Final Examination which will be conducted by State Board Examination

REPORT CARD:

  • Students weekend marks will be messaged every week via SMS or Calls
  • Students report card will be sent home once in a month
  • Students will be provided a copy of their marks
  • Parents can view their wards report on personal visit to the college. Our faculty members are always available to interact with the Parents/Gardians of the ward and get any information about his/her performance
REVISION:

Intermediate syllabus revision begins from 3rd week of January for Regular, MPC, BiPC and CEC from January 1st week onwards till February last week conducting surprise test on regular basis .
COUNSELLING:

  • On all working days from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM

    • DOUBTS CLARIFICATION:
    Students can get their doubts clarified from 3:00 PM to 4:30 PM everyday except on Friday.

    DURATION:
    Each class is for 50 minutes and 10 minutes break will be given after every 2 classes.

    LUNCH HOUR: 01:20 PM to 01:50 PM

    • Students must carry their lunch boxes.
    • Students are not allowed to leave the college premises during lunch hour.
    • Parents can drop their ward's lunch boxes at Front Office/Reception by writing ward's Name/Roll Number along with the box

    ABSENTISM:

    • In case of student's absence to college parents should inform to the college or must send a leave letter with the students on the next working day.
    • Student's absence will be informed to the parents on Phone/Send a Message to the number given for communication purpose
    NOTE: If necessary the college timings may be changed but it will be informed to the parents time to time.
